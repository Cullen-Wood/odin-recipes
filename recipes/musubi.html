Spam Musubi

A beloved snack in Hawaii, spam musubi is a quick and easy meal.

You only need a few ingredients and no technical knowhow, this snack

can be made in under an hour and the whole family will enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Spam
  • Rice
  • Seaweed
  • Sugar
  • Shoyu

Steps

  1. Cut spam into thin slices and place on foil
  2. Put a dash of sugar across the spam and a thin layer of soy sauce
  3. Bake at 350 for around
