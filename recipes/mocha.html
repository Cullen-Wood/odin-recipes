Mocha

You'll need an espresso machine for this recipe, but I love a good cup

in the morning before I head to class and hope you do too!

Ingredients

  • Coffee beans (whole)
  • Milk/milk alternative
  • Chocolate sauce
  • Whipped cream

Steps

  1. Grind 18g of coffee into your portafilter
  2. Use a distribution tool to evenly separate the grinds
  3. Tamp the coffee in the portafilter
  4. Place the portafilter into the group head
  5. Run the machine looking for a syrupy texture into a small shot glass
  6. Place your shot on the side and let it sit
  7. Pour milk into your milk jug just below the spout
  8. Steam your milk
  9. Pour your shot into a mug and then pour in the steamed milk
  10. Put in half an oz of chocolate syrup
  11. Place whipped cream on top and enjoy!
