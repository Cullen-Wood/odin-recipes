Chicken Stir Fry

This is a chicken stir fry, you can substitute any meat of your choice or tofu.

Any vegetables work but my personal preference is green beans and onion with the

occasional addition of zucchini.

Ingredients

  • Meat/Meat substitute
  • Vegetables of choice
  • Onion
  • Shoyu
  • Black pepper
  • Dash of salt
  • Corn Starch
  • Oyster Sauce

Steps

  1. Cut your vegetables and set aside on a plate
  2. Cut your meat/tofu into small cubes
  3. Place your meat into a large plastic bag
  4. Add the corn starch and shoyu into the bag with the meat
  5. Shake well and let reset for 15 minutes
  6. As the meat rests begin sauteeing the onion
  7. Take the onion out of the pan and set aside
  8. Cook the meat slightly browning
  9. Season the meat as it cooks
  10. Add the vegetables and more shoyu as well as oyster sauce
  11. Remove from heat and serve over rice
  12. Enjoy!
