Chicken Stir Fry

This is a chicken stir fry, you can substitute any meat of your choice or tofu.

Any vegetables work but my personal preference is green beans and onion with the

occasional addition of zucchini.

Ingredients

    Meat/Meat substitute Vegetables of choice Onion Shoyu Black pepper Dash of salt Corn Starch Oyster Sauce

Steps

    Cut your vegetables and set aside on a plate Cut your meat/tofu into small cubes Place your meat into a large plastic bag Add the corn starch and shoyu into the bag with the meat Shake well and let reset for 15 minutes As the meat rests begin sauteeing the onion Take the onion out of the pan and set aside Cook the meat slightly browning Season the meat as it cooks Add the vegetables and more shoyu as well as oyster sauce Remove from heat and serve over rice Enjoy!
